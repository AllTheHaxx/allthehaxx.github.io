AllTheHaxx

The better DDNet client by xush' and Henritees

Being based on DDNet, AllTheHaxx comes with all the cool features you're already familiar with.
If not, I highly recommand tring it out ;)

Additionally, this client provedes way more features that the original one. We also fixed bugs, that are not fixed in DDNet yet!




Features
~ Colorful Client: Colorize everything! Makes it look much cooler :)
~ Serverlist Updating: Update the infos without needing to refresh the entire list!
~ Serverlist Caching: Save the entire list to a file, which can be loaded on startup. This gets you rid of waiting for the serverlist on startup
~ Smooth style: Improvements to the UI to make it look cleaner + SMOOOOTH effects for quite anything! (e.g. scoreboard fading, vote displaying, etc.)
~ comes with additional KECK and KAZTE, brought to you by [0x539]
